#### Subspace
$$
V = p(F) = \{a_nx^n + \cdots + a_1x^1 + a_0 : n \in N, a_i \in F, i = 0,1 \cdots n \} \\
W = p_n(F) = \{a_nx^n + \cdots + a_0: n-is-fixed , a_i \in F, i=0,1 \cdots n \} \\
W \in V
$$
